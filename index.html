Investing in Stocks

The project analyzed various investment strategies using Modern Portfolio Theory (MPT) and momentum trading techniques, allocating a $100,000 investment among top-performing stocks and the S&P 500 index. Key findings revealed that the 'buy and hold' strategy for the top three stocks initially showed promising gains but ultimately mirrored the market's downward trend in 2022, resulting in the lowest returns when applied to the S&P 500 index. In contrast, the highest returns were achieved through momentum trading on the S&P 500. The volatility and unpredictability of the stock market in 2022, significantly influenced by the COVID-19 pandemic, adversely affected both strategies, underscoring the challenges of relying on traditional investment approaches during turbulent times.
